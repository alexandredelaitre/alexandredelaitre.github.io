About Me

Hi! My name is Alexandre Delaitre, I am 20 years old, currently in my 2nd Year at the University of Birmingham.

My main hobbies:
- Speedcubing (solving a Rubik's cube as fast as possible!) I have been attending competitions since 2016, since then I have competed in 14 different competitions including the World Championship in Paris and the UK Championship :). I have also had the opportunity to meet amazing people such as Erno Rubik (inventor of the Rubik's cube!), Mats Valk, Feliks Zemdegs (the best cubers in the world!). Results can be found: Here

- Programming, throughout my life I always loved solving problems so it was only a matter of time until I discovered programming, and once I did I knew it would stick! It initially started with me making games in Unity, but once I realised the infinite potential for human advancement in the future which could only be accelerated with computers, my focus shifted from developing games towards learning all about Artificial Intelligence, Web3 and all the technologies which will drive the future!
