somecoolai.com

somecoolai.com (or hireprep...) is a university team project, where we decided that as University students, we might be unlikely to have much interview practice until we actually get to that stage. So in order to prepare us (and everyone else!) we developed somecoolai.com which takes a job as a parameter and gives relevant questions which you might be asked and gives a reasonable score.

You can literally try it right here!

We use the Django framework to deliver the website and OpenAI's GPT-3 to form questions and rate/improve the answers. This was my first time working in a larger group and it was definitely interesting putting different development methodologies into practice! And whilst I don't think it's ideal to spend 6 weeks planning for a weekend programming challenge (thanks uni!), I definitely see the value in doing this for larger projects in the real world :).
